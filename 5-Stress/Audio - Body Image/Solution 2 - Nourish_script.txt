Nourish
Your body is your most valuable asset, and nourishing your body is essential for it to function at its best.
Here are a few ways you can do this:
Fuel it with good, wholesome food
Keep it hydrated
Move it in a way that feels good for you
Rest it when it's tired
Listen to it when it needs something
Recharge it through adequate sleep
Nourish your mind by being mindful of the media you consume
Speak to your body with love and respect
