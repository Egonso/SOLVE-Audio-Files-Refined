Know Your Worth
Self-confidence is a key component of assertiveness, and by practicing positive self-statements, you can boost your self-esteem and develop the confidence to assert yourself effectively.
Here’s how you can use positive self-statements:
Select a self-statement that resonates with you in an area where you’re seeking improvement.  For example: “I am worthy of respect”, or, “I communicate clearly at all times”
Repeat your chosen statements daily (and confidently) to reinforce their positive messages.
Create a mental image of yourself where your positive self-statement is true.
By incorporating positive self-statements into your daily routine, you can cultivate the self-confidence needed to assert yourself confidently in various situations.
