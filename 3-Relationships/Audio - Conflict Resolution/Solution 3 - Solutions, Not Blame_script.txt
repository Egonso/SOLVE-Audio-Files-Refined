Solutions, Not Blame
When things don’t go our way, it’s often easy to blame others instead of taking responsibility for our role in the situation.
But focusing on who’s to blame doesn’t help resolve the conflict.
Instead of dwelling on assigning fault, it’s important to concentrate on finding mutually beneficial solutions.
One effective way to do this is to highlight the common ground or shared goal between everyone involved.
We can do this using the following phrase: “Just like you, I also want…, how about we consider…”
